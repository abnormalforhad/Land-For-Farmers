Our Mission

Empowering farmers with decentralized data ownership and AI-driven agricultural intelligence

From the Fields to the Future

Agriculture is the backbone of civilization, yet farmers remain underserved when it comes to technology. We're changing that by creating a platform where every farmer can harness AI — without losing ownership of their data.

Using Shelby's decentralized storage, farm data is persistent, accessible, and owned by the farmers who create it. AI models deliver real-time insights — disease detection, yield prediction, irrigation planning — previously only available to large agribusiness.

Our pay-per-read marketplace enables farmers to monetize datasets while contributing to agricultural research globally.
